Assistant Professor
Department of Mathematics, Concordia College

I am currently an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at Concordia College. My interests include applications of commutative algebra to computational and theoretical neuroscience, neural coding, machine learning, and data-driven approaches.
I received my Ph.D. and M.S. in mathematics from the University of Nebraska - Lincoln. A couple years before that, I completed my undergraduate studies at the State University of New York at Oswego.
Outside of mathematics, I enjoy knitting, crocheting, baking, and playing video games.
